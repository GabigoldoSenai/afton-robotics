About us

William Afton, a renowned entrepreneur, extends his influence beyond business success through philanthropy, actively supporting educational programs. Afton Robotics, under his guidance, not only entertains but also serves as a beacon of inspiration for those pushing the boundaries of entertainment technology.


His commitment to excellence shines in the meticulous design and engineering of his animatronic creations, seamlessly blending advanced robotics with artistic creativity. Fortunately, just like his creations, he springs into action and always comes back with innovations!
